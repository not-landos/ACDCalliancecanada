Alliance of Canadian Defence Companies

Delivering sovereign Canadian capability for DND and the CAF

ACDC aligns Canada’s emerging defence firms to provide modern, scalable and export-ready systems—reducing dependence on foreign primes while accelerating adoption within the Canadian Armed Forces.

Mission

Strengthen Canadian-controlled defence delivery

ACDC will strengthen the ability of Canadian controlled defence companies to deliver modern, scalable and export ready capabilities to DND and the CAF. We directly support the federal objective of reducing reliance on American primes from roughly seventy-five percent to fifty percent by shifting capability delivery to Canadian industry.

75% → 50% Reduce foreign prime reliance
Export Ready Capabilities engineered for NATO markets
Canadian IP Protected and scaled domestically

Strategic Focus

Five priorities guiding the alliance

We are architecting a Canada-first defence economy—built on trusted IP, export readiness and coalitions that can stand shoulder-to-shoulder with any allied prime.

Canada first

ACDC concentrates investment, policy and technology roadmaps on home-soil capability. Every priority advances CAF readiness while elevating Canadian supply chains into NATO export lanes.

  • Shift procurement share toward Canadian integrators.
  • Protect domestic IP while accelerating export approvals.
  • Form coalitions that can stand up sovereign alternatives.

Core Functions

What the Alliance delivers

Policy & Procurement Reform

Technical recommendations on modernization, innovation pathways and capability planning submitted to DND, PSPC, ISED and central agencies.

Advocacy & Engagement

Coordinated lobbying for Buy Canada, flexible acquisition authorities and multi-year development frameworks.

Industry Events

Summits, workshops and showcases connecting companies, CAF operators, government officials and researchers.

National Ecosystem Development

Partnerships with academia, manufacturers, investors and northern & Indigenous communities to grow domestic capacity.
